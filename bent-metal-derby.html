How it works

Each round is a random arena: a derby (be the last car running), a 3-lap race on a jump-and-barrel track, or a 3-lap city circuit with tight 90° corners, sidewalks you can hop onto and solid buildings. Everyone races and rams.

Score by getting stuck in

Every hit you land and every car you wreck is worth points. In races you bank a share of those points by your finish: 1st 100% · 2nd 50% · 3rd 25% · 4th+ nothing. Surviving as the last car running banks the lot.
